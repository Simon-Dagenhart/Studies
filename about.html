About Maple Grove

32 years of educating curious, kind, and confident kids in the heart of Springfield.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide every child with a joyful, rigorous elementary education that builds strong readers, curious mathematicians, and kind community members — regardless of background or ability.

🔭

Our Vision

A school where every student leaves fifth grade confident in who they are, excited to keep learning, and equipped with the academic and social foundation to thrive in middle school and beyond.

Our story

A Brief History

  1. 1994

    Maple Grove Elementary is founded

    Opened with 120 students and six classrooms to serve the growing Springfield community.

  2. 2008

    New science & arts wing added

    A dedicated science lab, art studio, and music room expanded what we could offer students.

  3. 2016

    Named a State Blue Ribbon School

    Recognized for outstanding academic progress and closing achievement gaps.

  4. 2026

    New library wing opens

    Today we serve 480 students with a full library, makerspace, and expanded outdoor learning spaces.
