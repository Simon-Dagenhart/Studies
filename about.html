About Me

Hi, I'm Mr. Hamzah — a computer engineer as well as an English trainer and teacher, and I started Up Is To Go to bring both of those skill sets into one place, taught the way I wish more lessons were taught: patient, practical, and built around what you actually want to be able to do.

I teach at Al-Sindbad School and Cambridge Institution, alongside running Up Is To Go for students who want live, one-on-one lessons outside the classroom.

I believe the best lessons don't feel like lessons — they feel like steady, honest progress toward a goal that matters to you, whether that's holding a conversation with confidence or finally feeling at ease sitting down at a computer.

Alongside teaching, I also design and build websites for businesses — so if you run a business and need an online presence, that's something I can help with directly.

My Approach

  • Every course starts with a conversation about your goals, not a fixed syllabus.
  • Lessons are live and interactive — plenty of room for questions, mistakes, and real practice.
  • No jargon without explanation, whether we're discussing grammar or gigabytes.
  • Progress is paced to you, not a rigid schedule.

What I Teach

On the English side: conversation, grammar foundations, and business English. On the computer side: everyday computer basics, common software (documents, spreadsheets, email, video calls), and hardware fundamentals — what's actually inside your machine and how to troubleshoot the everyday stuff yourself.

Website Development for Businesses

Outside of teaching, I design and build websites for small and growing businesses — whether that's a straightforward online presence or something more tailored to how your business works. Get in touch if you'd like to talk through what you need.
