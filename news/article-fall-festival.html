Fall Festival Raises Record Funds for Playground Upgrade

May 10, 2026 · Written by the Maple Grove PTA

Maple Grove's annual Fall Festival brought out hundreds of families, staff, and neighbors for an afternoon of games, food, and community spirit — and raised a record $14,300 for the playground upgrade fund.

This year's festival featured a dunk tank (Principal Porter volunteered for three rounds), a cakewalk, face painting, three food trucks, and the now-legendary staff-versus-5th-grade tug-of-war, which ended, as always, with the teachers in the mud.

"Every dollar raised today goes straight toward new climbing equipment and shade structures for recess," said PTA president Elena Ruiz. "We could not do this without our families."

What the funds will support

  • A new accessible climbing structure for the K-2 playground
  • Shade sails over the main play area
  • Replacement of aging benches and picnic tables

Construction is expected to begin over winter break, with the upgraded playground ready in time for spring recess.

A huge thank-you to every volunteer, vendor, and family who made this year's festival the most successful yet. Save the date for next year's festival: October 2, 2027.
