Admissions & Enrollment

Enrolling at Maple Grove is simple. Here's everything you need to get your child started.

How it works

Enrollment Steps

  1. 1

    Confirm your school zone

    Check that your home address falls within the Maple Grove attendance zone, or apply for an open-enrollment transfer.

  2. 2

    Submit the enrollment form

    Complete the district's online enrollment form with your child's information and your household details.

  3. 3

    Provide required documents

    Upload or bring in proof of residency, birth certificate, and immunization records (see checklist below).

  4. 4

    Meet your teacher

    New families are invited to a welcome meet-and-greet with their child's teacher before the first day.

  5. 5

    First day of school!

    Families receive a welcome packet with supply lists, bus routes, and first-week schedules two weeks prior.

Before you apply

Required Documents

📄 Proof of Residency

A recent utility bill, lease, or mortgage statement showing your address within the school zone.

🎂 Birth Certificate

Official copy confirming your child meets the age requirement for their grade level.

💉 Immunization Records

Up-to-date records per state school requirements, or an approved exemption form.

📋 Previous School Records

For transferring students: most recent report card and any support/IEP plans, if applicable.

Common questions

Admissions FAQ

What age does my child need to be for kindergarten?

Children must turn 5 years old on or before September 1 of the enrollling school year.

Can I enroll if I live outside the school zone?

Yes — we accept a limited number of open-enrollment transfers each year, space permitting. Submit your request by May 1 for priority consideration.

Is before- and after-school care available?

Yes. Licensed care is available from 7:00 AM and until 6:00 PM on campus for an additional fee. See our Contact page to request more information.

How do you support students with an IEP or 504 plan?

Our special education team reviews all incoming plans before the first day and coordinates with families to make sure services begin immediately.

Can I tour the school before enrolling?

Absolutely — we offer campus tours every Tuesday and Thursday morning during the school year. Request one on our Contact page.

Questions about enrolling?

Our admissions office is happy to walk you through zoning, transfers, or anything else on your mind.
