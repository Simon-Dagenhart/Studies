Academics

A well-rounded K-5 curriculum built on strong fundamentals, hands-on discovery, and small class sizes.

Grade levels

Kindergarten Through 5th Grade

Every grade builds on the last, with consistent expectations and close teacher collaboration across levels.

🧸

Kindergarten – 1st Grade

Foundational phonics, number sense, and social-emotional skills through play-based, hands-on learning.

📗

2nd – 3rd Grade

Fluent reading and writing, multiplication and division, and an introduction to research projects.

📘

4th – 5th Grade

Deeper analysis in reading, multi-step math and pre-algebra readiness, and middle-school preparation.

Core subjects

Our Curriculum

📖

Reading & Writing

Balanced literacy blending phonics, guided reading groups, and daily independent writing time.

➗

Mathematics

Hands-on, problem-based math using manipulatives before moving to abstract practice.

🔬

Science

Weekly labs and experiments covering life, earth, and physical science standards.

🗺️

Social Studies

Local and world history, geography, and civics through projects and storytelling.

🎨

Art & Music

Weekly dedicated art and music instruction, plus an annual spring showcase for every grade.

🏃

Physical Education

Daily recess plus twice-weekly PE focused on teamwork, coordination, and healthy habits.

💻

Technology

Weekly computer lab time covering typing, digital citizenship, and beginner coding (K-5 friendly Scratch projects).

🌍

World Language

Introductory Spanish for all students starting in 2nd grade, twice weekly.

Support for every learner

Specialized Programs

🌟 Gifted & Enrichment

Pull-out enrichment groups and differentiated classroom projects for students ready to go deeper.

🧩 Special Education

Individualized support plans, a dedicated resource room, and inclusive co-taught classrooms.

🗣️ English Language Learners

Dedicated ESL instruction alongside full participation in general education classrooms.

🕐 After-School Programs

Homework club, robotics, chess, art studio, and a licensed after-care program until 6:00 PM.
